How it works

We suggest. You approve. We monitor.

No spreadsheets. No consultants. No guesswork. Vet INC brings your fee schedule to you on a schedule, suggests changes, and makes sure they work.

1
Connect your PMS

Upload your fee schedule from AVImark, Cornerstone, or any major PMS. Takes about five minutes.

2
We bring suggestions to you

On your schedule — quarterly, monthly, whatever works. Vet INC flags codes that have drifted, shows the suggested price, and explains why.

3
Approve or reject with one click

Review the suggestions. Approve what looks right, reject what doesn't. The whole thing takes minutes — not the multi-week project it used to be.

4
We monitor the results

After you make a change, Vet INC tracks revenue and visit volume to make sure it didn't hurt. If something looks off, we flag it so you can adjust.
