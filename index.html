The problem

Prices drift. Nobody has time to notice.

Costs go up every year. Most clinic prices don't keep pace — not from negligence, but from having 1,400 other things to manage.

📅

Years between updates

The average clinic hasn't touched most service codes in 18–36 months. In that time, costs have moved. Prices haven't.

📋

Too many codes to audit

A typical practice has 500–2,000 service codes. Nobody has the bandwidth to review them all, so they don't.

💸

Quiet revenue erosion

Pricing drift doesn't show up as a line item. It shows up as margin that quietly disappears, year over year.
