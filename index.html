The problem

Prices drift. Nobody notices until it's too late.

Costs go up every year — staff, drugs, equipment, supplies. Most clinic prices don't keep pace. Not from negligence. From having 1,400 other things to manage.

📅

Years between updates

The average clinic hasn't touched most service codes in 18–36 months. In that time, costs have moved. Prices haven't.

📋

Too many codes to audit

A typical practice has 500–2,000 service codes. Nobody has the bandwidth to review them all. So they don't.

💸

Quiet revenue erosion

Pricing drift doesn't show up as a line item on any report. It shows up as margin that quietly disappears year over year.

How it works

Three steps. Zero manual work.

Connect your practice management software once. Vet INC handles the rest.

Step 01

Connect your data

Upload your current price list from AVImark, Cornerstone, EzyVet, or any major PMS. Takes under five minutes. No IT required.

Step 02

We monitor everything

Vet INC continuously tracks your full service list — comparing against cost data, time since last update, and regional benchmarks. Automatically.

Step 03

You stay current

When something drifts, you get an alert with a clear suggestion. Approve it, adjust it, or let Autopilot handle it. Your call, always.
