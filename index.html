How it works

Pricing upkeep, on a schedule.

Connect once. Vet INC reviews your full price list on a regular cadence, so nothing drifts and nothing gets missed.

Step 01
Connect your clinic

Upload your service list from AVImark, Cornerstone, EzyVet, or any major PMS. Takes under five minutes. No IT required.

Step 02
We run regular reviews

On your schedule — weekly, monthly, or quarterly — Vet INC scans every code. It flags what's drifted and prepares suggested updates.

Step 03
Approve and stay current

Review the batch, approve what looks right, and your price list is updated. No spreadsheets. No back-and-forth. Done.

What we watch

Every code. Every review cycle.

Vet INC was built specifically for veterinary pricing — not adapted from generic software.

  • Full service list coverage

    All 500–2,000 of your codes are included in every review. Nothing gets skipped because it seemed minor.

  • What triggers a flag

    Time since last update, changes in your supply costs, and shifts in regional benchmarks. We surface only what actually needs attention.

  • Your PMS, connected

    AVImark, Cornerstone, EzyVet, Impromed, and more. No custom build required.

  • You stay in control

    Nothing changes without your approval unless you've explicitly configured Autopilot with your own thresholds.
