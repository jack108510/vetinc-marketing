How it works

Three steps. Zero manual work.

Connect your practice management software once. Vet INC handles everything else.

Step 01
Connect your data

Upload your price list from AVImark, Cornerstone, EzyVet, or any major PMS. Under five minutes. No IT involved.

Step 02
We monitor everything

Vet INC tracks your full service list continuously — comparing cost data, time elapsed, and regional benchmarks.

When something drifts, you get a clear suggestion. Approve it, adjust it, or let Autopilot handle it. Your call.
