How it works

Set your schedule. We handle the rest.

Connect once. Vet INC runs on your cadence — weekly, monthly, or quarterly — and makes sure nothing gets missed.

Step 01
Connect your clinic

Upload your service list from AVImark, Cornerstone, EzyVet, or any major PMS. Under five minutes. No IT required.

Step 02
Reviews run on schedule

Every code is scanned on your chosen cadence. Vet INC flags what's drifted and lines up suggested updates with context.

Step 03
Approve and stay current

Review the batch, approve what looks right, done. No spreadsheets, no consultants, no back-and-forth.

Plans

As hands-on as you want to be.

You review everything.

Most popular Co-pilot

Autopilot

  • Monthly change report
  • Override anytime
